ABOUT US / FACULTY

Faculty






A faculty of close to 40 educators has been carefully selected on the basis of their subject expertise and compassion for children. Their competence is constantly enhanced through regular exposure to workshops and seminars on recent teaching technology and pedagogical advancement. The faculty has been formally trained in the preparation of animated lessons in all subjects on the computer. They attended a workshop on ‘Excellence in Teaching’ to enhance their professional capabilities.
