KINGSWAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL




A Community of Growth

At KINGSWAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, we empower students to ask insightful questions, explore disciplinary boundaries and confront conventional ways of thinking. Check out our site to lear more about all that we have to offer. We strive for total Quality Education for all our students, Working on all dimensions of a child's development academic, Physical, Creative, moral & spiritual. The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. The Central Board of Secondary Education is well known and is recognized not only in India but in many other countries as well. The Standards of educational curriculum as laid down by the Board are carefully designed by NCERT (The National Council for Educational Research & Training), keeping in view the real need of an average student. They are primarily meant to promote the student's intelligence, grasp and sense of curiosity. The Curriculum is widely recognized as most suitable for competitive examinations designed by UPSC & other examining bodies.
