ABOUT US / INTRODUCTION

Introduction






The School has stood by the values of its illustrious founders. We strive for total Quality Education for all our students, Working on all dimensions of a child's development academic, Physical, Creative, moral & spiritual. The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. The Central Board of Secondary Education is well known and is recognized not only in India but in many other countries as well. The Standards of educational curriculum as laid down by the Board are carefully designed by NCERT (The National Council for Educational Research & Training), keeping in view the real need of an average student. They are primarily meant to promote the student's intelligence, grasp and sense of curiosity. The Curriculum is widely recognized as most suitable for competitive examinations designed by UPSC & other examining bodies.







special features

The School follows workshop method of instruction & students are encouraged to learn by doing practically.

The School has highly qualified and competent staff. All the teaching staff is trained and well versed in their subjects.

The School also maintains a comprehensive library with a large number of titles covering every field of knowledge. The books for the library are selected after consolation with the school staff and other eminent educationists. The library follows an open shelf system to allow the student wide reading choice.

The School also provides facilities for sports like Basket Ball, Volley Ball, Athletics, Badminton, Lawn Tennis, Cricket, Hockey, Gymnastics, Roller & Inline Skating, Taekwondo and other traditional and modern outdoor games. Annual Sports Week is held every year and winners are awarded medals & certificates on house basis.

An Annual function is organized every year in October/November to game an opportunity to the students to take part in extra curricular activities and develop their personalities. Students of the dramatics and art. Art and Craft are taught on a regular basis. Plays and Cultural functions are organized on the Annual Day and national festivals. Debates and Elocution contests are also held periodically

Absence without prior permission of the Principal on application will not be allowed. Students remaining absent without adegvati reason of sickness will be penalized. Application should be submitted before the assembly time. Parents should make sure that their wards are regular and punctual to the school. Irregular attendance, unjustified or unexplained absence for more than 3 days, disobedience or objectionable moral influence justify dismissal

Regular Hobby classes in the field of Dance, Theatre, Art, Personality Development, Ikebana, Electronics, Pottery, Music, Gardening, Creative Writing, Ornithology, etc. are held.
